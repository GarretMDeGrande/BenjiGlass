Introduction

Benji Glass, also known as Ben Brock, is an independent glass blower who has been immersed in the industry as a young adult. Growing up around Mary Janes House of Glass, he developed a keen interest in the art form.

Passion for Glass Blowing

Benji's passion for glass blowing truly ignited in his late 20s when he began experimenting with various techniques and styles, and learning from other experienced glass blowers. Inspired by the beauty and versatility of glass, he dedicated himself to honing his craft and exploring new possibilities within the medium, he even setup is own little studio. His art is inspiration colors comes from the Zebra Strip Chew Gum Benji. has collabed on many pieces and hopes to bring happiness and art to you.
